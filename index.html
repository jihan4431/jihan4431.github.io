About

I am a PhD student in the Department of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Xingyi Yang and Prof. Ajay Kumar.

Prior to this, I obtained my BSc degree in Computer Science from Soochow University and my MSc degree in Computer Science from Sichuan University, where I was advised by Prof. Yanan Sun.

My current research focuses on World Models, Video Generation, and Image Generation. Previously, I mainly focused on Neural Architecture Search.
